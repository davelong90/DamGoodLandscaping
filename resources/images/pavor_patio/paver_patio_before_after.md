Paver Patio Before and After Portfolio item
 ==========================================

The Problem
 -----------------------------------------
Client wanted extra usable outdoor space in the front of the house and reduce maintenance of another garden bed.

The Solution
 -----------------------------------------
Maintained the current pathway leading to the door to maintain budget, and used the garden bed to create a paver patio.

The Result
 -----------------------------------------
The client was very pleased with the results which gave them extra seating in the front of the house. They appreciated the creative thinking to maintain
budget and still provide the requirements they were after.
